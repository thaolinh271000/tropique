About Us

'TROPIQUE'

A TROPICAL BOUTIQUE HOTEL

Our hotel is situated along the Nha Trang coastline, offering a space that is both simple enough for moments of relaxation, equipped enough for complete happiness, and private enough for emotions to settle. Tropique is an unforgettable spot for you and your family to enjoy a vacation in the midst of lush nature, from grassy fields and shady trees to graceful coconut palms. It's a place to return to tranquility.

  • Our Hotel

    ‘Tropique’ is a cozy luxury hotel located in Nha Trang, Vietnam. Our goal is simple: we offer a peaceful, comfy, and serene retreat away from busy city life.

    Tucked in nature's embrace, our small hotel focuses on giving you a quiet, relaxed space to unwind and recharge. We're all about making sure you feel at home, away from the noise and crowds. Our aim is to provide a calming escape, where you can enjoy comfy rooms, exclusive amenities, and the beauty of nature, along with some special extras.

  • Location

    Nha Trang, the idyllic coastal gem of Vietnam, cradles the exquisite Tropique Hotel within its embrace. With its sun-drenched beaches lapped by the azure waves of the East Vietnam Sea, Nha Trang sets the perfect stage for Tropique's tranquil retreat.

    This tropical haven, nestled between verdant mountains and the sparkling ocean, invites guests to immerse themselves in a sanctuary where luxury meets the natural beauty of Vietnam's captivating landscapes. Whether you're basking in the sun-dappled shores, exploring vibrant coral reefs, or indulging in the modern amenities of Tropique Hotel, Nha Trang promises a harmonious blend of relaxation and adventure.
