Nha Trang, the idyllic coastal gem of Vietnam, cradles the exquisite Tropique Hotel within its embrace. With its sun-drenched beaches lapped by the azure waves of the East Vietnam Sea, Nha Trang sets the perfect stage for Tropique's tranquil retreat. This tropical haven, nestled between verdant mountains and the sparkling ocean, invites guests to immerse themselves in a sanctuary where luxury meets the natural beauty of Vietnam's captivating landscapes. Whether you're basking in the sun-dappled shores, exploring vibrant coral reefs, or indulging in the modern amenities of Tropique Hotel, Nha Trang promises a harmonious blend of relaxation and adventure.

As the sun paints the skies with hues of warmth, Nha Trang unveils its cultural tapestry. Temples steeped in history, French colonial remnants, and the vibrant pulse of Vietnamese life converge in a cityscape that mirrors the diverse charm of Tropique itself. From the bustling seafood markets to the tranquil boat cruises, every corner of Nha Trang echoes the promise of an enriching escape. Tropique Hotel, with its commitment to luxury and tranquility, becomes not just a destination but a seamless part of Nha Trang's vibrant narrative, inviting travelers to discover the perfect synthesis of natural beauty, cultural exploration, and coastal indulgence.

Arrival from Cam Ranh Airport

Distance: 38 kilometers

Travel time: 40 minutes

Instruction:

  • Exit the airport and head southwest toward Route 657.
  • Turn right onto Route 657 and continue for approximately 10 kilometers.
  • At the roundabout, take the second exit onto Tran Phu Street.
  • Follow Tran Phu Street for about 5 kilometers until you reach the hotel located on the right-hand side.
  • Tropique will be easily visible and accessible from Tran Phu Street.
