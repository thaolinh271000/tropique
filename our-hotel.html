Our Hotel

‘Tropique’ is a cozy luxury hotel located in Nha Trang, Vietnam. Our goal is simple: we offer a peaceful, comfy, and serene retreat away from busy city life.

Tucked in nature's embrace, our small hotel focuses on giving you a quiet, relaxed space to unwind and recharge. We're all about making sure you feel at home, away from the noise and crowds. Our aim is to provide a calming escape, where you can enjoy comfy rooms, exclusive amenities, and the beauty of nature, along with some special extras.

Tropique offers comfort, privacy, and nature's tranquility. We pride ourselves on providing a luxurious stay with personalized services that cater to your every need. Experience the serene beauty of Vietnam's landscapes, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Unwind amidst lush surroundings and engage in a variety of activities, from thrilling water sports to relaxing spa treatments. Whether you seek adventure or simply want to unwind, our hotel promises an unforgettable stay filled with moments of joy and relaxation for all our guests.

Founded in 2016, Tropique emerged as a tranquil haven nestled in the breathtaking landscapes of Vietnam. Envisioned as a luxurious retreat, the hotel has continuously redefined hospitality, seamlessly blending comfort, personalized service, and a commitment to environmental responsibility. From its serene beginnings, Tropique has expanded, faced global challenges with resilience, and pivoted towards community support, embodying a holistic approach to hospitality and sustainability.

  • 2016

    The Founding

    Tropique was established in 2016, envisioned as a serene retreat within Vietnam's breathtaking landscapes, prioritizing luxury, and tranquility for its guests.

    Since its establishment in 2016, Tropique has evolved into a symbol of refined escape. Growing from its serene beginnings, we've stayed true to our commitment to provide a haven of comfort and personalized service. With each passing year, we continue to redefine hospitality, offering an unforgettable journey for our cherished guests.

  • 2018

    Expansion Begins

    Just two years later, Tropique saw its first expansion, adding more rooms and enhancing its services to accommodate a growing number of visitors seeking solace and rejuvenation.

    This expansion not only increased our capacity but also allowed us to introduce new features, from upgraded amenities to enhanced recreational offerings. As a result, Tropique became a more comprehensive and inviting destination for those seeking a tranquil escape amidst the beauty of Vietnam's landscapes.

  • 2020

    Pandemic Challenge

    In 2020, the hotel faced the global challenge of the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite the setbacks, Tropique remained steadfast in ensuring the safety of its guests and staff while adapting to the changing circumstances.

    During this challenging period, we implemented rigorous health and safety measures, including enhanced sanitation protocols and social distancing practices, to create a secure environment. Tropique also utilized innovative technologies for virtual experiences, ensuring guests could enjoy our hospitality safely from their homes. This resilience marked a pivotal moment in our commitment to community well-being and adapting hospitality for changing times.

  • 2022

    Sustainability Focus

    Continuing its commitment to environmental responsibility, Tropique intensified its efforts toward sustainability, promoting eco-friendly practices and conservation measures despite the challenges posed by the pandemic. These ranged from reduced plastic usage to active involvement in local biodiversity preservation projects, reflecting a resilient commitment to environmental responsibility.

    The hotel also undertook extensive energy-saving measures, incorporating renewable energy sources and adopting innovative technologies to reduce its ecological footprint. Additionally, collaborative partnerships with local communities were forged, fostering a holistic approach to sustainability that extended beyond the hotel's immediate operations.

  • 2023

    Community Resilience

    By 2023, Tropique pivoted to support the local community, offering aid, resources, and opportunities to uplift and contribute positively to the area, reinforcing the spirit of resilience during difficult times.

    The hotel actively engaged in partnerships with local businesses and initiatives that aligned with its vision of community development. Educational programs, vocational training, and skill development workshops were implemented, empowering individuals to thrive in challenging circumstances.
