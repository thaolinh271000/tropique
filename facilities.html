Facilities

Welcome to Tropique Hotel, where luxury meets convenience, and every aspect of your stay is meticulously curated for your utmost comfort. Our array of world-class facilities promises to elevate your experience, catering to both leisure and business needs.

Indulge in our exclusive Villa Pools, a serene oasis where tranquility and relaxation converge. Immerse yourself in crystal-clear waters surrounded by lush greenery, offering a secluded retreat for ultimate rejuvenation.

Stay active and invigorated at our state-of-the-art GYM & Fitness center, equipped with top-of-the-line amenities and expert trainers to help you maintain your wellness routine during your stay.

Pamper your senses at our luxurious SPA, where a range of rejuvenating treatments awaits, designed to soothe your body and soul, leaving you feeling refreshed and revitalized.

For business or social gatherings, our sophisticated Meeting Rooms provide the perfect venue, equipped with modern technology and impeccable service to ensure your events are successful and memorable.

At Tropique Hotel, our facilities are crafted to cater to your every need, promising an exquisite blend of relaxation, fitness, wellness, and convenience throughout your stay.

  • Villa Pools

    Designed to exceed your expectations, our Villa Pools are more than just a place to swim; they are a sanctuary where time stands still. Whether you seek a refreshing morning dip, a sun-kissed afternoon lounging on a private cabana, or a leisurely evening under the stars, these pools offer an idyllic escape from the ordinary.

  • GYM & Fitness

    Immerse yourself in a world of health and vitality as our experienced trainers stand ready to assist and guide you toward achieving your fitness goals. Whether you seek a rigorous workout or prefer a more relaxed exercise regimen, our GYM & Fitness center provides the perfect environment to rejuvenate both mind and body.

  • SPA

    Nestled amidst verdant tropical gardens, our SPA offers a sanctuary of serenity, evoking the natural beauty and harmony synonymous with our locale. Drawing from indigenous botanicals, aromatic herbs, and local fruits, our curated treatments promise an authentic and revitalizing experience.

  • Meeting Room

    Nestled within our tropical oasis, our versatile venue offers an ideal blend of professionalism and relaxation. With state-of-the-art amenities and a serene ambiance, our Meeting Room allows you to conduct business in a tranquil setting surrounded by the beauty of our lush surroundings.
